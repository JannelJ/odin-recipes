Miso Honeyed Tofu with Mushroom Rice

Indulge in the perfect balance of savory and sweet with our Miso Honeyed Tofu paired with fragrant Mushroom Rice.
Tender tofu marinated in a delightful blend of miso and honey, served alongside aromatic rice infused with earthy mushrooms.

A harmonious fusion of flavors that promises a satisfying culinary experience in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pak Choi
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • 150g Chestnut Mushrooms
  • 225g Smoked Tofu
  • 10g Cornflour
  • 150g Jasmine Rice
  • 300ml Water for the Rice
  • 30g Miso Paste
  • 30g Honey
  • 25ml Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Trim the root from the bok choy then thinly slice widthways. Thinly slice the chestnut mushrooms. Peel and grate the garlic.

  2. Drain the tofu and cut into 2cm cubes. Pop the cubes of tofu into a large bowl and season with salt and pepper.
    Add the cornflour and shake the tofu in the bowl to cover evenly.

  3. Pour the rice in a saucepan with a 1/4 tsp of salt. Cover with the lid and bring to boil. Leave to cook for 10 mins.

  4. Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan and fry the mushrooms until golden, 4-5 mins. Stiry in the pak choi and allow to soften slightly, 1-2mins.
    Add the garlic, stir together and cook for 1 min more. Transfer the vegetables to a bowl and set aside.

  5. Wipe out the pan and put back on high heat with a drizzle of oil. When the oil is hot, add the tofy and fry until golden, 3-4 mins.
    Meanwhile, mix the honey and miso together in a small bowl.

  6. Once the tofu is golden all over, allow the pan to cool for a minute or so. Add the honey and miso mix into the pan.
    As it begins to bubble, stir the tofu in the miso to glaze all over, 1-2 mins. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it up with a fork
    and stir through your mushroom and pak choi. Pour in the soy sauce and mix evenly, 1-2 mins.

  7. Divide the mushroom rice between your bowls and top woth crispy miso tofu. Enjoy!
