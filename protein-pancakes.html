Easy Protein Pancakes

Up your protein with these pancakes made with eggs, oats, milk and protein powder. A great breakfast after a workout, enjoy with nut butter or fruit

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 75g Oats
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 2tbsp milk
  • 1tbsp Baking Powder
  • A Pinch of Cinnamon
  • A Scoop of Protein Powder
  • Flavourless oil for frying
  • Maple Syrup and Blueberries to serve

Steps

  1. Whizz the banana, oats, eggs, milk, baking powder, cinnamon and protein powder in a blender for 1-2 mins until smooth.
    Check the oats have broken down. If not, blend for another minute.

  2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan. Ladle in 2-3 rounds of batter, leaving a little space between each to spread.
    Cook for 1-2 mins, until bubbles start to appear on the surface and the underside is golden.
    Flip over and cook for another minute. Repeat with the remaining batter. Serve in stacks with maple syrup and fruit.
